Transportation

From Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport 从广州白云国际机场

• 地铁：乘地铁3号线（番禺广场方向）到客村站 → 换乘地铁8号线（滘心方向）到中大站，进入中山大学南校区南门。
• 出租车/网约车：直接前往中山大学南校区（北门或南门），车程约50–70分钟。

• Metro: Take Line 3 towards Panyu Square 番禺广场 to Kecun Station 客村站 → Transfer to Line 8 towards Jiaoxin 滘心 to Sun Yat-sen University Station 中大站. Enter South Gate of SYSU South Campus.
• Taxi/Ride-hailing: Direct to SYSU South Campus (South Gate or North Gate), about 50–70 minutes.

From Guangzhou South Railway Station 从广州南站

• 地铁：乘坐地铁2号线（嘉禾望岗方向）到昌岗站 → 换乘地铁8号线（万胜围方向）到中大站，进入中山大学南校区南门。
• 出租车/网约车：直接前往中山大学南校区（北门或南门），车程约30–40分钟。

• Metro: Take Line 2 towards Jiahewanggang 嘉禾望岗 to Changgang Station 昌岗站 → Transfer to Line 8 towards Wanshengwei 万胜围 to Sun Yat-sen University Station 中大站. Enter South Gate of SYSU South Campus.
• Taxi/Ride-hailing: Direct to SYSU South Campus (South Gate or North Gate), about 30–40 minutes.
